Tasks generally require picking up objects at their locations and placing them in the robot's kit.

Some notes about the tasks:

  • Task objects will only ever be at their designated locations; never at another location.
  • The environment will not be static, and other agents (for example, me) might change it from time-to-time.
  • Tasks that cannot be completed without a physical intervention by a human within the arena must be aborted.

Some notes about the LARGE_GEAR:

  • The LARGE_GEAR must be 'machined' in the SCHUNK machine before it can be placed in the robot's kit.

  • The LARGE_GEAR must be grabbed along the diameter of its head in order allow for successful gear insertion into the SCHUNK chuck. (example correct grasp in the image)
